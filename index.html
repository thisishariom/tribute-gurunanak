About

About

Guru Nanak is the first of the ten Sikh Gurus.
He is said to have travelled far and wide across Asia teaching people the message of ik onkar (ੴ, 'one God'), who dwells in every one of his creations and constitutes the eternal Truth. With this concept, he would set up a unique spiritual, social, and political platform based on equality, fraternal love, goodness, and virtue.

Quotes

Objective

Let sincere faith be your bowing in prayer, and let the conquest of your mind be your objective in life.

Karma

Karma is the tree, the Lord's Name the branches, Dharmic faith the flowers, and spiritual wisdom the fruit.

Destiny

According to the karma of past actions, one's destiny unfolds, even though everyone wants to be so lucky.

Love

True love shall not be broken, if the True Guru is met.

Servent

One who makes the Lord's humble servent suffer, shall himself suffer and rot.

Jealousy

Jealousy and envy bring terrible pain, and one is cursed throughout the three worlds.

Wisdom

In the realm of wisdom, spiritual wisdom reigns supreme.

Egotism

Do not indulge in egotism, and dwell within your own mind; the Knower Himself knows everything.
